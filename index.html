Year to Turns Calculator

This calculator determines the number of turns it takes to reach a specific year in RFC Dawn of Civilization, based on the selected game speed and starting year.

To use the calculator, select your desired game speed and enter your starting year (the default is -3000). Then, input one or more target years between -3000 and 2025, separated by commas, semicolons or spaces. The results will show the number of turns it takes to reach each target year from the starting year.

Please note that it is not always possible to determine the exact turn. In such cases, the two closest turns are displayed.
